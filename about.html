SINCE 1990


Quickly and globally with the advantages of freshness,
convenience and unique flavor Da Kasi originated in Taiwan

in Beijing Road in April 1999.

It mainly operates fresh tea drinks in Taiwan, and is popular
in Yangcheng with its advantages of freshness, convenience and
unique flavor, and has quickly opened branches throughout the
country. In order to meet the increasing demands of customers for

with the joy of trying to be the most loved one. After more than
